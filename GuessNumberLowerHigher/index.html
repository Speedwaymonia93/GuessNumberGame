Welcome to the guessing number game

The rules are simple:

  • The game has 30 rounds
  • Each time you have to guess if the next dice number will be higher or lower
  • For each correct guess you will gain 0.1 point

In the upper right corner you will see your current game progeress and the number of remaining rounds

You can close the app at any time and the progrss will be saved
